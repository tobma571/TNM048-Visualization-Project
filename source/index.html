Youtube Trends

Tree Map description:

The Tree Map displays the distribution of genres among the 400 monthly most viewed YouTube videos. You can filter the results by region and time. By clicking one of the categories, the most viewed videos from this category are shown. Once a category is chosen, the videos are sorted by most viewed, starting at the top left. Click anywhere inside the Tree Map to return to the previous view.

Tag Cloud Description:

The Tag Cloud displays the most popular tags in the trending videos on youtube. You can filter the results by country, time, and by category, by selecting a category in the Tree Map to the left.
